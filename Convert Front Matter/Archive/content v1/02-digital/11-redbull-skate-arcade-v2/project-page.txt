Red Bull Skate Arcade is an online skateboarding competition. Players have to film themselves doing tricks then they upload their videos through the site. Each tricks are reviewed by a professional skateboarder jury who gives points to the best players. the biggest chalenge was the video upload process on mobile / desktop to ensure the best performance in any conditions.
